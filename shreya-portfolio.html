Final Year Project 📄 Paper Submitted AI/ML Research
Protein Language Model-Based Molecular Pattern Analysis for Alzheimer's Disease

Developed a novel unsupervised PLM-based framework to analyze APP isoforms at a molecular level for Alzheimer's research. Used ESM-2 embeddings, PCA & UMAP dimensionality reduction, and K-Means clustering to identify that the KPI domain is the primary structural driver differentiating isoforms — achieving a silhouette score of 0.7742 with perfect ARI/NMI stability (1.0). Identified APP305 as a critical outlier and validated results via ablation studies. Supervised by Dr. Baisakhi Chakraborty, NIT Durgapur. Forwarded for publication (May 2026).
